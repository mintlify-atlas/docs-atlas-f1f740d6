PvZ Portable is a community-driven, open-source reimplementation of **Plants vs. Zombies: Game of the Year Edition**. Built on SDL2 and OpenGL ES 2.0, it delivers an authentic GOTY experience on virtually every modern platform — from your desktop to your Nintendo Switch.

<Warning>
  PvZ Portable does **not** include any copyrighted game assets. You must supply your own `main.pak` and `properties/` folder from a legally purchased copy of Plants vs. Zombies GOTY Edition.
</Warning>

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Truly cross-platform" icon="globe">
    Runs on Linux, Windows, macOS, Android, iOS, WebAssembly, Nintendo Switch, and more. Supports x86_64, aarch64, riscv64, loongarch64, and other CPU architectures.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Authentic gameplay" icon="gamepad">
    Nearly 100% faithful recreation of the GOTY Edition experience, including all game modes, the Zen Garden, and mini-games.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Portable saves" icon="floppy-disk">
    The `.v4` save format lets you transfer mid-level saves between platforms — share a save between Linux, Android, and Switch seamlessly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multilingual" icon="language">
    Supports English, Chinese, German, Spanish, French, and Italian game resource packages from official GOTY editions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Play in your browser

No build needed — open the [WebAssembly build](https://wszqkzqk.github.io/pvz-portable-wasm/pvz-portable.html) directly in your browser. Load your own game resources (ZIP or folder) and start playing. All files stay local; nothing is uploaded to any server.
