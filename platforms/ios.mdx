<Info>
  PvZ Portable does not include game assets. You must provide `main.pak` and the `properties/` folder from a legally purchased copy of Plants vs. Zombies: GOTY Edition. See the [game assets guide](/game-assets) for details.
</Info>

## Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
| ----------- | ------- |
| iOS / iPadOS version | 15.0 or later |
| Architecture | arm64 |

## Getting the IPA

Download `pvz-portable-ios-arm64.ipa` (unsigned) from the [GitHub Releases page](https://github.com/wszqkzqk/PvZ-Portable/releases).

Because the IPA is unsigned, it must be sideloaded onto your device. Choose a method below.

## Sideloading methods

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="AltStore">
    1. Install [AltStore](https://altstore.io/) on your Mac or PC and then on your device.
    2. Open AltStore on your device and tap **My Apps** → **+**.
    3. Select the downloaded `.ipa` file.
    4. AltStore signs and installs the app using your Apple ID.

    <Warning>
      Free Apple ID signatures expire after 7 days. You must refresh the app in AltStore before expiry or it will stop launching.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="TrollStore">
    [TrollStore](https://github.com/opa334/TrollStore) supports **iOS 14.0–16.6.1** and installs apps permanently without signature expiry.

    1. Install TrollStore on your device following the official TrollStore documentation for your iOS version.
    2. Transfer the `.ipa` file to your device (via AirDrop, Files app, or a file transfer tool).
    3. Open the `.ipa` in TrollStore and tap **Install**.

    <Note>
      TrollStore installs are permanent and do not expire. No Apple ID is required.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Xcode with free Apple ID">
    1. Open Xcode on your Mac and sign in with your Apple ID under **Settings** → **Accounts**.
    2. Connect your device and trust the Mac on the device.
    3. Create a new single-view Xcode project, set the bundle identifier to something unique, and select your free personal team.
    4. Replace the built app with PvZ Portable, or use a tool such as `ios-deploy` or `ideviceinstaller` to install the `.ipa` directly.

    <Warning>
      Free Apple ID signatures expire after 7 days. You must rebuild and reinstall before expiry.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Importing game resources

The app's Documents folder is accessible via iTunes/Finder file sharing and the iOS Files app. Transfer `main.pak` and the `properties/` folder into the app's Documents directory.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iTunes / Finder">
    1. Connect your device to your Mac or PC.
    2. Open Finder (macOS Catalina and later) or iTunes (Windows / older macOS).
    3. Select your device, go to **Files**, and find **PvZ Portable** in the app list.
    4. Drag `main.pak` and the `properties/` folder into the app's document area.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="iOS Files app">
    1. Open the **Files** app on your device.
    2. Navigate to **On My iPhone/iPad** → **PvZ Portable**.
    3. Copy or move `main.pak` and the `properties/` folder into this directory.

    You can transfer files to your device first via AirDrop, iCloud Drive, or any other method supported by the Files app.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

After placing the files, launch PvZ Portable. The game reads resources from its Documents directory on startup.

## Input

SDL2 automatically maps touch events to mouse input, so the game is fully playable with a touchscreen. The original game's 4:3 aspect ratio and mouse-based input model are preserved — no touch-specific UI optimizations have been made, matching the behavior of the Android port.

## Building from source

You can build the unsigned IPA yourself using the included build script. Requirements: Xcode 15+, CMake 3.21+, and vcpkg with `VCPKG_ROOT` set.

```bash
./ios/build-ios.sh Release
```

The script configures CMake with the `arm64-ios` vcpkg triplet and the `ios/ports` overlay, builds with Xcode, and produces `build-ios/pvz-portable-ios.ipa`. See the [build overview](/building/overview) for general CMake background.
